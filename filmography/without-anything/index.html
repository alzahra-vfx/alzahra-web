Without Anything

Director :Mohsen Gharaie

Release date:February 2, 2021

A remote village has not had a previous boom for years. Residents have either been forced to migrate or are in poverty and misery, but the village has a new guest. But the village has a new guest who was born here. Twenty years have passed since her migration, he is wealthy, and the people dream that he has come to save the village from this misery.
